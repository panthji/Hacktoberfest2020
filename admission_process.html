Admission process

Indian Institue of Information Technology, Una

To understand the admission process we make a list of steps of admission process, you need to follow these steps at time of admission.

REGISTRATION

At reporting center , first of all you need to REGISTRATION.

DOCUMENT VERIFICATION

After Registration you need to verify your documents.

FEES VERIFICAION

Once Documents verification is done , now you need to provide fees payment verification.

DATA ENTRY

Then there is also a small process as DATA ENTRY, and your Admission will be confirmed.

HOSTEL ALLOTMENT

In last you need to complete HOSTEL ALLOTMENT process, as hostel is compalsory for all students.
